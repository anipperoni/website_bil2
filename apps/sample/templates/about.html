OUR MISSION

Fostering Innovation in Brunei

Brunei Innovation Lab (BIL) is the nation's premier platform for fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and technological advancement. We connect businesses, startups, researchers, and government to build a thriving innovation ecosystem.

Through our three pillars - Innovation Showcase, Innovate Brunei, and Capability Accelerator - we're developing the talent and technology that will drive Brunei's future economy.

OUR VISION

A Brunei where innovation drives economic diversification and sustainable growth

We envision a future where Bruneian innovators and entrepreneurs are globally competitive, creating homegrown solutions that address local and regional challenges while contributing to the nation's economic transformation.

Our Three Pillars

Innovation Showcase

A platform to demonstrate cutting-edge technologies and solutions developed in Brunei and beyond. We connect innovators with potential partners, investors, and customers.

Innovate Brunei

Our flagship development programme supporting startups and entrepreneurs on their journey from idea to market. Includes accelerator programs, mentorship, and funding support.

Capability Accelerator

Addressing competency gaps in emerging technology areas through targeted training, workshops, and resources. Building the skills Brunei needs for the digital economy.

Governance Structure

BIL is guided by a steering committee comprising representatives from our founding partners.

Steering Committee

The committee provides strategic direction and oversight, ensuring BIL's programs align with national innovation priorities and the interests of all stakeholders.

Operations Team

Day-to-day operations are managed by a dedicated team responsible for program delivery, community engagement, and ecosystem development.
