We believe in builders

BIL started with a simple idea: give people a place to turn their ideas into real things. No complicated processes, no gatekeeping - just a space to work, people to learn from, and a community that gets it.

Since 2013, we've been helping startups launch, running workshops, hosting hackathons, and generally trying to make Brunei a better place for anyone who wants to build something.

What we're trying to do

Brunei has smart, creative people with great ideas. We want to help them actually make those ideas happen - whether that's building a tech startup, learning new skills, or just meeting others who think the same way.

We're not here to be corporate. We're here to be useful.

The stuff we do

Help startups grow

Accelerator programs, mentorship, connections to investors - the support you need to go from idea to actual business.

Teach useful skills

Workshops on coding, design, business - the practical stuff that actually helps you build things.

Bring people together

Hackathons, meetups, and events where you can meet others who are building cool stuff.
