ABOUT THE EVENT

What is Startup673?

Startup673 is Brunei's flagship national hackathon, bringing together innovators from all backgrounds to solve real-world challenges in just 48 hours. Named after Brunei's country code (+673), it represents our commitment to homegrown innovation.

Whether you're a developer, designer, business mind, or just have a great idea, Startup673 is your launchpad to turn concepts into reality.
